Let's Play Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Rules to the game

1. You will be playing against Lima.

2. Choose between Rock, Paper, and Scissors.

3. The first one to three points wins.

Here are the rules to getting a point in the game:

  • Rock beat Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

Note: If You and Lima choose the same option [e.g. Paper and Paper], then no one gets the point.
